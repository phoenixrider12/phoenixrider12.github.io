Hi, I’m Aryaman! I am a PhD student at Stanford University in the Department of Aeronautics and Astronautics, where I feel grateful to be advised by Prof. Somil Bansal in the SIA Lab. Prior to Stanford, I spent four wonderful years at IIT (BHU) Varanasi earning my B.Tech. in Electronics Engineering.

My research focuses on learning from deployment-time data to uncover failure patterns in robotic systems for continuous improvement. I aim to: (a) bake safety mechanisms into their design, and (b) ensure reliable and efficient operation at runtime through lightweight guardrails.

You can read a short overview of my research here, grab my resume, or find my work on Google Scholar. Feel free to reach out — I’m always happy to chat about robotics, safety, and learning-based control.
